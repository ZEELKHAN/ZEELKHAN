About Book

"The Poetry of Me" by Zeel Khan is a poignant collection of poems that delves into the unspoken emotions and suppressed feelings that hide beneath the surface of human connection. This evocative book invites readers on a journey of self-discovery, exploring the complexities of love, loss, and the human experience. Learn More

Who is Zeel Khan?

Zeel Khan is a 16-year-old poet, dreamer, and student from Surat, Gujarat, who balances her love for words with her academic pursuits in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM). While her days are often filled with solving equations and unraveling scientific concepts, her heart belongs to the world of creativity and expression. Learn More
